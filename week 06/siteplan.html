Site Name

GM. — Gabriel Muñoz Portfolio

The site name uses the initials GM followed by a period, which functions as both a typographic logo and a professional identifier. The period signals precision and finality — qualities relevant to fraud analysis and systematic development work. The domain idea is gabrielmunoz.dev, combining a personal identifier with the .dev TLD that directly signals the technical nature of the work.

Site Purpose

This portfolio presents Gabriel Muñoz as a professional candidate to recruiters and hiring managers in tech and data roles. It documents work across three domains: workflow automation (Power Automate), data analysis (Excel, Power BI, Chart.js), and web/software development (HTML/CSS/JS, C#, SQL). The site communicates technical depth, professional context (Banco Guayaquil), and ongoing education (BYU-Idaho), giving visitors everything they need to evaluate fit without requiring a resume download.

Scenarios

Questions a target visitor (recruiter / tech hiring manager) might ask:

"What kind of automation work has this person actually shipped — is it toy projects or real systems used in production?"
"Does he know SQL and data fundamentals, or just Excel and dashboards?"
"Can I see examples of his code or output files, and how do I contact him quickly?"
